Off The Break

Off The Break is a text-based fantasy paintball game built in Java. Draft a team of real NXL players, compete against AI-controlled teams, and battle through a four-team tournament to reach the finals.

Features
Draft your own team of 5 NXL players
Play through 3 preliminary matches
Top 2 teams advance to the championship match
Each match consists of 5 rounds
Choose from different plays and strategies
AI-controlled opponents
Live match commentary and score updates
